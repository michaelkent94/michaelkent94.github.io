Hello interested blog reader!

I write about programming: my personal projects, things that seem interesting to me, maybe some news here and there. I want to provide tutorial-like information on some topics and simply my opinions and insights on others.

My name is Michael Kent and I'm a Software Developer. You may have noticed the Fred Brooks quote in the sidebar. That pretty much sums up how I feel about what I do. I love exploring new concepts, ideas, and technologies with software.

Here's that same quote with a bit more at the end
